Aufgabe 1.3.9  
Vereinfachen Sie die folgenden Terme für geeignete Zahlen a,b,x,y,z:
  1. 3x-6x y2 +4xyz -2x  =  .


    3x-6x y2 +4xyz -2x   =  - 3 2 +3 y2 -2yz .



  2. (3a-2b)·(4a-6)  =  .


    (3a-2b)·(4a-6)  =  12 a2 -18a-8ab+12b .



  3. (2a+3b )2 -(3a-2b )2  =  .


    (2a+3b )2 -(3a-2b )2 = (4 a2 +12ab+9 b2 )-(9 a2 -12ab+4 b2 )  = -5 a2 +24ab+5 b2  .



  4. 3a 3a+6b + 2b a+2b  =  .


    3a 3a+6b + 2b a+2b   =   a a+2b + 2b a+2b   =   a+2b a+2b   =  1 .



In den Eingaben dürfen keine Klammern mehr auftreten.

  3. ( a2 +5a-2)(2 a2 -3a-9)-( 1 2 a2 +3a-5)( a2 -4a+3)  =  .

    = 2 a4 +10 a3 -4 a2 -3 a3 -15 a2 +6a-9 a2 -45a+18       -( 1 2 a4 +3 a3 -5 a2 -2 a3 -12 a2 +20a+ 3 2 a2 +9a-15)    = 3 2 a4 +6 a3 - 25 2 a2 -68a+33 .

  2. 972  =  .


    972   =  (90+7 )2   =   902 +2·90·7+ 72   =  8100+1260+49  =  9409 .



  3. 412 - 382  =  .

  1. 4 x2 +12xy+9 y2  =  .


    4 x2 +12xy+9 y2   =  (2x+3y )2  .



  2. 64 a2 -96a+36  =  .


    64 a2 -96a+36  =  (8a-6 )2  .



  3. 25 x2 -16 y2 +15x+12y  =  .


    25 x2 -16 y2 +15x+12y = (5x )2 -(4y )2 +3·(5x+4y)  = (5x+4y)(5x-4y)+3·(5x+4y)  = (5x+4y)(5x-4y+3) .
